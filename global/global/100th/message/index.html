Yokohama Rubber was established in Yokohama City, Kanagawa Prefecture, on October 13, 1917. Over the past 100 years, we have delivered a wide variety of products to the world that include tires, industrial products and golf products based on manufacturing that is infused with our unique spirit and technology. We have always held dear the importance of domestic production of highly functional rubber products indispensable to the growth of Japan, which has enabled us to contribute to the happiness and well-being of individuals and society as a whole. None of this, however, would have been possible without all of our stakeholders, from customers and business partners to shareholders and local communities, and we’d like to express our most sincere appreciation for your support over the years.

It’s because of everyone that we’ve made it this far to celebrate 100 years in business. Going forward, we’re also committed to continuing to work together in our endeavor to “deliver the best products at competitive prices and on time,” our mission as a manufacturer since our earliest days, so that we grow into a global company with a distinctive presence in terms of corporate value and market position.


Tadanobu Nagumo
Chairman and Representative Member of the Board
